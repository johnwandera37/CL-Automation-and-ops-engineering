```
**Step 3 — Assemble your deployment folder**

After building, your `dist/` folder will have the two exes. Create a clean folder like `KRA_Installer\` and put these in it:
```
KRA_Installer/
├── kra_checker.exe          ← from dist/
├── heartbeat_monitor.exe    ← from dist/
├── install.exe
├── uninstall.exe
├── config.json              ← leave station_name and anydesk_code blank
├── credentials.json         ← your Google service account key
├── anydesk_detector.py
└── fetch_station_info.py
